VOLUNTEERING FOR STUDENTS

OUR VALUES

Through a Human-Centred Approach , Engineers Without Borders (EWB) , aim to give everyone,specifically those in poverty, access to engineering knowledge and skills which will in-turn improve their communities health and wellbeing and also lead to a fairer and more equitable future for all.

Ways to Volunteer

UNIVERSITY CHAPTER

The university chapters are centred around a learning institution, an organisation or a geographic area, and extends EWB’s presence beyond the staff team. Chapters provide a framework for all types of engineers  . They can have participants who are currently studying all the way to being retired .University chapters are a great way to learn more about human -centred engineering which is something that we aim to share through this organisation.

NATIONAL OFFICE PLACEMENTS

National Office Placements allow you to get first hand experience with working as a part of our national office team. This job allows you to support all the work happening through their delivery and further development.

MORE INFO

SCHOOL OUTREACH

With over 300 participating schools and 20000 student participants , School outreach is all about inspiring students from primary to year 12 to further explore STEM subjects and skills in the future through delivering workshops in the classroom.
